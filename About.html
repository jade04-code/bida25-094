LEARN ABOUT OUR LOANS

Emergency loan

Emergency loan is meant to cover urgent and unplanned costs like medical bills, emergency travel or emergency repairs. These loans are frequently handled quicker than typical loans as they are supposed to offer fast financial aid.

Personal loan

The personal loan is more general and can be used in numerous ways like paying school fees, consolidating debt, meeting home payments or even making important purchases. Personal loans tend to be unsecured (they often do not have any collateral).

Salary loan

A salary loan is a short to medium-term loan that is aimed at employed individuals who have a steady monthly income. Lenders typically evaluate the ability of the borrower to repay in terms of fixed monthly payments and instead subtract repayments.
